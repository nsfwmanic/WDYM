The Grey family. The first family. The original nobles.

They sacrificed themselves to put an end to the great evil.

The Grey have a dark power inside of them. They actually created dark magic, and death of their blood line was the only way to put an end to the great darkness.

The princess of the Grey and her party went on a quest to defeat her brother who used their dark gift as a evil magic to rule the land and every other one. He was stopped and the power was sealed away. Dark magic in bits of pieces survived, and that darkness lasted for a long time.
